PixelBox Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 27, 2025

TL;DR: PixelBox doesn't collect, store, or share any of your data. Everything stays on your device. We can't see what you create because we don't have servers.

Information Collection

PixelBox collects ZERO personal information.

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any data about you or your usage of the app. This includes:

  • No personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • No location data
  • No usage analytics
  • No crash reports
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No tracking

Local Storage Only

All your creations and game data are stored locally on your device using iOS local storage. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not backed up to any servers
  • Is only accessible by you
  • Will be deleted if you uninstall the app

No Third-Party Services

PixelBox does not use any third-party services, including:

  • No analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • No advertising networks
  • No social media integrations
  • No cloud services

No Internet Required

PixelBox works completely offline. The app does not require an internet connection to function and does not make any network requests.

Children's Privacy

PixelBox is safe for children of all ages. Since we don't collect any data, there is no risk to children's privacy. Parents can be confident that their children's information is completely safe while using this app.

Data Security

Since we don't collect any data, there is no data to secure. Your creations are stored locally on your device and protected by iOS's built-in security measures.

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • All data stays on your device
  • Delete saved worlds anytime from within the app
  • Uninstall the app to remove all data permanently

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this privacy policy (for example, if we add optional features that use data), we will update this page and notify users through the App Store update notes.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us:

  • GitHub: github.com/ninjaboy/pixelbox/issues
  • Website: ninjaboy.github.io/pixelbox

Summary

PixelBox is built with privacy as a core principle. We don't want your data, we don't need your data, and we don't collect your data. Your creations are yours alone.
